Welcome to Oummal

Oummal is the reflection of a vision driven by a sense of justice and equity. Our vision is to empower people to take ownership of their rights through a bottom-up approach. Since our registration in 2013, Oummal has been dedicated to advancing the rights and well-being of all beings, human and animal alike.

MedLeb

Paws Rescue Program

Rescued over 720 animals since inception, with an average of 80 pets rescued annually for the past 9 years. The program’s efforts have also spayed and released approximately 360 dogs into safe environments.

Urgency Center Expansion

Expanded the Urgency Center to accommodate an additional 20 dogs per year, increasing capacity to 200 dogs annually.

Legal Support and Recognition

Conducted research and legal support efforts that helped over 500 stateless individuals gain recognition and access to basic rights.

Empowering Youth Through Vocational Training

Trained over 250 youth in vocational skills, with a 45% job placement rate, contributing to reducing unemployment and filling skill gaps in Lebanon’s job market.

Paws Rescue Program

About Program

The Paws Rescue Program at Oummal reflects our commitment to animal welfare and the tireless efforts of our dedicated team. Through rescue, rehabilitation, medical care, and community outreach, we strive to create a compassionate society where the rights and well-being of all animals are respected and protected. Our ongoing efforts and future goals aim to enhance the lives of the animals we care for and to promote a culture of humane treatment for all creatures.

Oummal over the years

In 2013, we established Oummal as an NGO with a focus on promoting justice and equity in Lebanon.

2014

Launch of the Oummal Mutual Fund, which provided comprehensive health coverage to small business owners and self-employed workers.

2017

Activated the Oummal Capacity building program to address the challenges of youth unemployment and skill gaps in the job market. Hundreds of trainees ended up with a job.

2018

Establishment of the Universal Health Coverage National Observatory, a key initiative aimed at improving health coverage by mediating between patients, hospitals, and public guarantors.

2021

Management of the National COVID-19 Vaccination Hotline (1214), which successfully handled over 900,000 calls, addressing various grievances and providing crucial support during the pandemic.

2022

Even the all-powerful Pointing has no control about the blind texts it is an almost unorthographic life

2023

Development and implementation of the MedLeb software (MedLeb Patient guide mobile app and MedLeb Pharmacy Service web app) to digitalize drug-related procedures at the Ministry of Public Health, enhancing transparency and efficiency in Lebanon’s healthcare sector.

2024

Development of the Maktoum AID network and application for stateless people, service providers and NGOs and networks targeting the issue on a multiapproach level.
