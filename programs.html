Maktoum AID Program

About Program

The Maktoum AID app aims to facilitate the registration of stateless individuals, foster connections, collect data, and raise awareness. It supports three types of users: Guests, organizations/activists, and stateless individuals. Guests can explore information on statelessness by country, while organizations can create profiles, share projects, and conduct surveys or polls. Stateless individuals can register, access resources, receive notifications, and communicate with organizations through a secure platform.

Paws Rescue Program

About Program

The Paws Rescue Program at Oummal reflects our commitment to animal welfare and the tireless efforts of our dedicated team. Through rescue, rehabilitation, medical care, and community outreach, we strive to create a compassionate society where the rights and well-being of all animals are respected and protected. Our ongoing efforts and future goals aim to enhance the lives of the animals we care for and to promote a culture of humane treatment for all creatures.

About Us

Oummal is the reflection of a vision driven by a sense of justice and equity. Our vision is to empower people to take ownership of their rights through a bottom-up approach. Since our registration in 2013, Oummal has been dedicated to advancing the rights and well-being of all beings, human and animal alike.
