About Us

Who Are We?

A journey of resilience, innovation, and impact.

The Beginning

A group of passionate young individuals from diverse backgrounds came together in 2010 to tackle the health coverage crisis they had personally endured.

Their vision? To provide healthcare solutions for workers and self-employed individuals who lacked formal health coverage. And so, Oummal was born – a name reflecting their commitment to laborers and fairness.

The Mutual Fund - 2014

Oummal launched the Mutual Fund in 2014, addressing healthcare access gaps for small business owners and self-employed workers.

  • Affordable monthly subscriptions for public health access.
  • 50% coverage for radiographs & lab tests.
  • Collaboration with hospitals & Ministry of Public Health.

Fighting Hospital Violations

Reports flooded in from non-affiliated patients, exposing violations in hospitals – including denied admissions & forced pre-payments.

Oummal launched a three-year investigation (2014-2017), proving that workers were entitled to free public healthcare without external funding.

2019 - A National Healthcare Revolution

Lebanon lacked a dedicated patient rights platform. Oummal created the National Observatory for Universal Health Coverage to balance the scales.

  • Hotline #2424 for hospital complaints.
  • Filed legal cases against non-compliant hospitals.
  • Partnered with civil society, media & government to push reforms.

1214 Hotline & COVID-19 Response

Amid the COVID-19 pandemic, Oummal was entrusted by the Ministry of Public Health to manage the 1214 hotline for vaccinations.

With only 6 employees, the team handled over 6,000 calls in just 3 days. Despite challenges, Oummal built:

  • A custom electronic tracking system for cases.
  • Forms to simplify early vaccination requests.
  • Support teams for vaccination centers.

About Us

Oummal is the reflection of a vision driven by a sense of justice and equity. Our vision is to empower people to take ownership of their rights through a bottom-up approach. Since our registration in 2013, Oummal has been dedicated to advancing the rights and well-being of all beings, human and animal alike.
